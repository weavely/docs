---
title: "Password Protection"
---

Need to ensure that only a select group of people can fill in your form or survey? Weavely allows you to password protect your form. Here's how to enable this:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to your form's settings">
    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot showing how to navigate to the settings of a Weavely form.](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-03-at-18.41.56.webp)
    </Frame>

    Open your form from the Weavely dashboard, navigate to the "**Settings"** tab at the top of the editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Access settings">
    <Frame>
      ![Screenshot showing how to enable password protection in Weavely AI forms.](/images/Screenshot-2026-06-03-at-18.59.15.webp)
    </Frame>

    Under the "**Access**" sub menu, you'll find a "**Password protect**" toggle. When enabled you'll be able to specify the password which respondents need to enter to continue to your form
  </Step>
</Steps>

When you password protect your form, visitors will be greeted with a screen such as the one shown below.
