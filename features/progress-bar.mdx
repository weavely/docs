Want to show your respondent where they are in your survey or form? Enable progress bars for your Weavely forms by following these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title='Step 1: Enable "Show Progress Bar"' icon="bars-progress" titleSize="h2">
    Navigate to your form's "**_Settings-\>General_**" tab and toggle the "Show Progress Bar" option as shown below.

    ![Screenshot showing how to enable progress bars in Weavely AI](/images/Screenshot2025-12-15at06.51.14.webp)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step 2: Customise the Progress Bar's Color (Optional)" icon="palette" titleSize="h2">
    The progress bar uses your form's "**_Primary color_**", which you can either change by opening the "Theme" panel on the right or asking our AI.

    ![Screenshot showing a progress bar in a Weavely AI form](/images/Screenshot2025-12-15at10.30.38.webp)
  </Step>
</Steps>
